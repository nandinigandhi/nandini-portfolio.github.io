Hey! I am nandini , currently a third year graphic design student at OCAD University based in Toronto. I coded this website from scratch because I wanted to make the space my work is displayed in a vessel. I am interested in generative art, web—development, creative programming, typeface design, typography, layout design, user interface design and visual identity systems. I am always eager to acquire and contribute new skills and experience from a new environment with a passionate mindset. I am also interested in design tools, archives, design philosophy, music and general semantics.
