For this project I was tasked with creating an annotated bibliographt. This exploration delves into the captivating world of color psychology within the context of digital media. The study investigates how different colors are pivotal in shap- ing user experiences, conveying brand identities, and eliciting specific emotional responses. Through a curated collection of images and thoughtful analysis, this work aims to comprehensively understand the strategic and artistic use of color in the digital landscape. The introduction sets the stage for the significance of color in design, emphasizing its impact on user perception, visual identity systems, and overall engagement in the digital realm.
